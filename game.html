1. Use ↑, ←, ↓,→ to control the spacecraft, press Space to Fire. 2. The initial fuel value is 45 and it decrements by 1 point per second. When the fuel value is 0, the game ends. 3. Touching off the fuel bottle can increase 15 points, the maximum value is 45 points. 4. Add 5 points to hit enemy ships. The planet needs to hit twice, adding 10 points. Deduct 10 points for hitting allies. 5. If you hit the enemy, you lose 15 points of fuel, and if you hit your friend, you deduct 10 points. 6. The game allows negative points. 7. Press P to pause the game, press M to mute.
